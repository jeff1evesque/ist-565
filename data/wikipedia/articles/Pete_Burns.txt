In 1980 changes were made to the band's line-up and the name was changed to Dead or Alive. Burns was vocalist and songwriter. The band rose to mainstream success with their 1984 single "You Spin Me Round (Like a Record)". He rose to further celebrity status in the British media following his appearance on Celebrity Big Brother 4, in which he finished in fifth place. He appeared on further television reality shows, including as a presenter. Burns had a powerful singing voice and was known for his ever-changing, often androgynous appearance, which he freely admitted was greatly modified by cosmetic surgery.
