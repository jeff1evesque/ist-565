Yuzvendra Chahal (born 23 July 1990) is an Indian cricketer who represents India in both One Day Internationals and Twenty20 Internationals. He plays for Haryana in Indian domestic cricket and Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League. He is a leg break bowler.
Chahal along with Ajantha Mendis, is one of only two players in T20I history to have taken a 6 wicket haul.
