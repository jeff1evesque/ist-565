Subha Nagalakshmi Munchetty-Chendriah (born on 25 February 1975) is a British Indian television presenter and journalist. She regularly presented BBC One's BBC World News, BBC Breakfast and Victoria Derbyshire. She is also a former presenter of BBC Two's weekday financial affairs programme Working Lunch. In January 2017, she temporarily left Breakfast to provide maternity cover for Newsnight as their acting business editor.
