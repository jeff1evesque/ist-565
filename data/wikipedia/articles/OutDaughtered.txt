OutDaughtered is an American reality series on TLC starring Adam and Danielle Busby, parents of the only all-girl quintuplets in the U.S. They also have a seven-year-old daughter, Blayke Louise. The series first aired on May 10, 2016. The series was renewed for a second season. The second season premiered on November 15, 2016. The series was renewed for a third season on January 17, 2017.
