Vijay Deverakonda (born Vijay Sai Deverakonda 9 May 1989) is an Indian film actor known for his works in Telugu cinema.  Vijay gained recognition through the 2016 romantic comedy Pelli Choopulu which won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu and the Filmfare Award for Best Film - Telugu. Vijay was then starred in Arjun Reddy (2017), and Mahanati (2018). His performance in Arjun Reddy, won him awards such as including the Filmfare Award for Best Actor - Telugu.
