Lee Joon-gi (born 17 April 1982) is a South Korean actor, singer and model. He rose to fame on his first leading role playing a clown in the film The King and the Clown (2005). Since then, he has starred in television series My Girl (2005), Iljimae (2008), Arang and the Magistrate (2012) Scholar Who Walks The Night (2015) and Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo (2016).
