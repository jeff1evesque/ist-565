Mary Anne Trump (née MacLeod, Scottish Gaelic: Màiri Anna NicLeòid; May 10, 1912 – August 7, 2000) was a Scottish-born American immigrant who was the mother of Donald Trump, the 45th and current President of the United States. Born in the Outer Hebrides, she emigrated in 1930 and became a U.S. citizen in 1942. As the wife of real estate developer Fred Trump she raised five children and engaged in philanthropic activities in the New York area.
