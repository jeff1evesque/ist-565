Binibining Pilipinas 2017, was the 54th edition of Binibining Pilipinas, that took place at the Smart Araneta Coliseum in Quezon City, Philippines on April 30, 2017. Miss Universe 2016 Top 6 Maxine Medina crowned her successor Rachel Louise Obregon Peters as Miss Universe Philippines 2017 at the end of the event while Miss International 2016 Kylie Verzosa crowned Maria Angelica "Mariel" Andolong de Leon as Binibining Pilipinas-International. The pageant also awarded four other titles to participate in minor international beauty pageants in which Miss Intercontinental 2016 Top 15 Jennifer Hammond crowned Katarina Sonja Artigas  Rodriguez as Binibining Pilipinas-Intercontinental, Miss Supranational 2016 Top 25 Joanna Louise Eden crowned Chanel Olive Villamayor Thomas as Binibining Pilipinas-Supranational, Miss Grand International 2016 1st Runner-up Nicole Cordoves crowned Elizabeth Dorado Clench as Binibining Pilipinas-Grand International and Miss Globe 2016 3rd Runner-up Nichole Marie Manalo crowned Nelda Cunan Ibe as Binibining Pilipinas-Globe. Named as first and second runners-up, respectively were Charmaine Francisco Elima, 24, a flight attendant from Rizal; and Kristel Nones Guelos, 24, a dentist from Tanauan City.This pageant marks a number of firsts in its 54-year history: the number of semifinalists was narrowed to 25 and the winner of the fan votes via text will receive the People's Choice Award that will make her the last to be called in the Top 15.
