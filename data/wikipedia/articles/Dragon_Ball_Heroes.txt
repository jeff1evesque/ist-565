Dragon Ball Heroes (Japanese: ドラゴンボール ヒーローズ, Hepburn: Doragon Bōru Hirozu) is a Japanese trading arcade card game based on the Dragon Ball franchise. It debuted on 11 November 2010. In 2016, an update launched that improved the user experience in the form of enhanced graphics and easier accessibility of characters. The update was named Super Dragon Ball Heroes (スーパー ドラゴンボールヒーローズ).
