Maria Sakkari (Greek: Μαρία Σάκκαρη; born 25 July 1995) is a Greek professional tennis player. She achieved her career-high singles ranking of No. 31 on 6 August 2018. Her highest WTA doubles ranking is No. 293, achieved on 16 November 2015.

Her mother Angelikí Kanellopoúlou was also a top 50 player.
