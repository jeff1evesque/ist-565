Sushant Singh Rajput (born 21 January 1986) is an emerging polymath, an Indian film and televisionactor and an entrepreneur. He started his career with television serials, including an award-winning performance in the soap opera Pavitra Rishta (2009â€“2011) and as a participant in two dance reality shows. He then entered films, playing one of the three male leads in the drama Kai Po Che! (2013), for which he received critical acclaim as well as three Best Male Debut awards. His other notable roles since then have included the male lead of the romantic comedy Shuddh Desi Romance (2014) and the titular detective in the thriller Detective Byomkesh Bakshy! (2015). In 2016, Rajput appeared in sports film M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story, in which he essayed the role of Indian cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni. The film was a commercial success and earned him critical acclaim.
Niti Ayog, an Indian government body, signs him recently to promote the Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP).
