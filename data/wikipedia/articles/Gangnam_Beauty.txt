Gangnam Beauty (Hangul: 내 아이디는 강남미인; RR: Nae Aidineun Gangnammiin; lit. My ID is Gangnam Beauty) is a 2018 South Korean television series starring Im Soo-hyang, Cha Eun-woo, Jo Woo-ri and Kwak Dong-yeon. The series is based on the webtoon of the same name published in 2016 by Naver Webtoon. It premiered on JTBC on July 27, 2018 and airs every Friday and Saturday at 23:00 (KST).
