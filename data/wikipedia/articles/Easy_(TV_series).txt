Easy is an American comedy-drama anthology series written, directed, edited and produced by Joe Swanberg. It consists of sixteen half-hour episodes. The series is set in Chicago. The first season was released on Netflix on September 22, 2016.In April 2017, Swanberg revealed the series had been renewed for a second season, which was released on December 1, 2017. In August 2018, the series was renewed for a third and final season.
