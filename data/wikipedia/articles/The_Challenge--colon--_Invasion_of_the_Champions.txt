The Challenge: Invasion of the Champions (originally promoted and titled as The Challenge: Invasion) is the 29th season of the MTV reality game show, The Challenge. The new individual format was filmed in Krabi, Thailand during October and November 2016, and features alumni from Real World, Road Rules, The Challenge, and Are You the One? competing.
The season premiered on February 7, 2017, and concluded with the reunion special on May 16, 2017.
