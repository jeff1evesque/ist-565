Park Hyung-sik (born November 16, 1991), known mononymously as Hyungsik, is a South Korean singer, actor and  dancer. He is a member of the South Korean boy group, ZE:A and its sub-group ZE:A Five. As an actor, he is known for his roles in The Heirs (2013) High Society (2015), Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth (2016),  Strong Girl Bong-soon (2017) and Suits (2018).
