Tiger Shroff (born Jai Hemant Shroff on 2 March 1990) is an Indian actor who works in Hindi films. The son of actor Jackie Shroff and producer Ayesha Dutt, he made his film debut with a leading role in the 2014 action comedy Heropanti, which earned him a Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut nomination.Shroff went on to star in the action drama Baaghi (2016), which earned over ₹1.3 billion (US$19 million), and went on to star in its successful sequel Baaghi 2 (2018), which earned over ₹2.5 billion (US$36 million) worldwide, despite generally negative reviews from critics.
