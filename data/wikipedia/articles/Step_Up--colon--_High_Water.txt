Step Up: High Water is an American drama web television series based on the Step Up film series created by Duane Adler that premiered on January 31, 2018 on YouTube Red (now YouTube Premium).
The series was created by Holly Sorensen, who also serves as an executive producer alongside Channing Tatum and Jenna Dewan-Tatum. On May 22, 2018, it was announced that YouTube had renewed the series for a second season.
