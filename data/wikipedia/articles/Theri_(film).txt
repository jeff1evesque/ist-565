Theri (English: Spark) is a 2016 Indian Tamil language, action - thriller film written and directed by Atlee and produced by Kalaipuli S. Thanu. The film features Vijay and  Samantha Ruth Prabhu in the lead roles, while  Amy Jackson, Mahendran, Prabhu and Raadhika amongst others form an ensemble cast. Featuring music composed by G. V. Prakash Kumar and cinematography handled by George C. Williams.
The film had a worldwide release on 14 April 2016. It was also dubbed into Telugu and was released as Policeodu (English: Police Man) on 15 April 2016, however, the title was later on altered to Police and remade in Telugu with Ravi Teja.The film was later dubbed into Hindi under the same name by Goldmines Telefilms in 2017.
