Force 2  is a 2016 Indian action thriller film written and directed by Abhinay Deo, which features John Abraham and Sonakshi Sinha in the lead roles. It is a sequel to the 2011 film Force, and the second installment of the Force film series. Principal photography of the film commenced in August 2015, and the film was released on 18 November 2016. Upon release, the film received positive reviews.
