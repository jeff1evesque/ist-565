Dark is a German science fiction thriller web series co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. It is the first German-language Netflix original series and debuted on Netflix's streaming service on December 1, 2017. The first season received mostly positive reviews from critics. It has been compared and contrasted to the other Netflix series Stranger Things. A second season has been approved by Netflix with production to begin in 2018.
