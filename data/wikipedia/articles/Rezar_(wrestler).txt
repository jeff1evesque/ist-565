Gzim Selmani (born 16 June 1994) also known as ‘The Albanian Psycho’ is a Albanian professional wrestler, former mixed martial artist for Bellator MMA and Bamma UK and a World Pro Kickboxing heavyweight champion. He was the heavyweight champion for kickboxing league RDL. He is currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the Raw brand under the ring name Rezar along with Akam as The Authors of Pain. He previously competed in WWE's developmental system, NXT, where he achieved status as an NXT Tag Team Champion and Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic winner (with Akam).
