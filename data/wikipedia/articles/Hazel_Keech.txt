Gurbasant Kaur (born Hazel Keech; 1986/1987 (age 31–32)) is a British−Mauritian model who has appeared in Indian television programs and films. She has appeared in Billa and Bodyguard as well as in a Suzuki advertisement. She danced in the Frankfinn Music remix item number, "Kahin Pe Nigahaen". She appeared in the reality television programme Bigg Boss 7 in 2013.
