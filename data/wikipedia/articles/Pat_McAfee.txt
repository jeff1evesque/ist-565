Patrick Justin McAfee (born May 2, 1987) is an American Pro wrestling analyst, comedian and former American football punter who played eight seasons for the Indianapolis Colts of the National Football League (NFL). He was a placekicker and punter at West Virginia and was drafted by the Colts in the seventh round of the 2009 NFL Draft. McAfee retired from football in February 2017 and joined the satirical sports blog Barstool Sports, along with appearing on the WWE's NXT TakeOver: New Orleans panel in April 2018 and NXT TakeOver: Chicago II in June 2018.
