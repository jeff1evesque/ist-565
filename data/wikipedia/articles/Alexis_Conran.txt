Alexis Conran (or Alex Conran) is a British Actor, writer and TV presenter, best known for co-hosting the BBC Three show The Real Hustle from 2006 until 2012. He was born in South Paris, France, and moved to Greece when he was a  child. Alexis won Celebrity Masterchef 2016 on 29 July beating Louise Minchin and Jimmy Osmond in the final.
Alex is of Greek heritage.
