Rahat Indori (Urdu:  ڈاکٹر راحت اندوری  ‬‎) (Hindi: डॉ. राहत इन्दोरी ) (born 1 January 1950) is one of the most acclaimed celebrity Urdu poet and a bollywood lyricist. Prior to this he was a pedagogist of Urdu literature at Indore University. Rahat Indori is very well known and loved amongst his millions of fans worldwide for his poetic brilliance and a very peculiar style of rendering ash'aars.
