Elisabeth Marie "Lisa" Haydon (born 17 June 1986) is an Indian model, fashion designer, and actress, who mainly appears in Hindi films. Haydon made her acting debut with a supporting role in the 2010 romantic comedy Aisha and received critical praise for her performance in the comedy-drama Queen, which garnered wide recognition and a Best Supporting Actress nomination at Filmfare, among other accolades. Haydon later starred in the commercially successful romantic comedy Housefull 3 and had a brief role in the Karan Johar-directed romantic drama Ae Dil Hai Mushkil (both 2016).
She is a model in India and internationally. She appears on several popular Magazine covers such as Grazia (India), Cosmopolitan (magazine), Maxim, Elle (India), Verve (magazine), Vogue India, Femina (India), FHM (magazine), Hello! (magazine), and L'Officiel. She is known for her Rampwalk appearance at Lakme Fashion Week, the no. 1 fashion show in India.
