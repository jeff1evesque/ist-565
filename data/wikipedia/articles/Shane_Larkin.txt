DeShane Davis "Shane" Larkin (born October 2, 1992) is an American professional basketball player for Anadolu Efes of the Turkish Basketball Super League and the EuroLeague. He played college basketball for the University of Miami. He was selected by the Atlanta Hawks with the 18th overall pick in the 2013 NBA draft, where he was immediately traded to the Dallas Mavericks. Larkin is the son of Baseball Hall of Famer Barry Larkin.
