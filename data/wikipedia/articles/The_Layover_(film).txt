The Layover is a 2017 American sex comedy film starring Alexandra Daddario, Kate Upton, Matt Barr, Matt L. Jones, Rob Corddry, Kal Penn, Molly Shannon, and William H. Macy. Macy also directed the film, which was written by David Hornsby and Lance Krall. Filming began early May 2015 in Vancouver, BC and was completed early June 2015.
The film was released on August 3, 2017, through DirecTV Cinema prior to being released in a limited release and video on demand on September 1, 2017, by Vertical Entertainment.
