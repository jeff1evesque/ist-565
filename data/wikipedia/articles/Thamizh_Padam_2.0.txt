Tamizh Padam 2 (Tamil Film 2) is a 2018 Indian Tamil language parody film, written and directed by C. S. Amudhan, and produced by S. Sashikanth. A sequel to Thamizh Padam (2010), the film stars Shiva and Disha Pandey, with music composed by Kannan and cinematography by Gopi Amarnath. The film had its theatrical release on 12 July 2018.
