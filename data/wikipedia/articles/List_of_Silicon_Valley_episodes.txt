Silicon Valley is an American television sitcom created by Mike Judge, John Altschuler and Dave Krinsky. The series focuses on five young men who found a startup company in Silicon Valley. The series premiered on April 6, 2014, on HBO.As of  May 13, 2018, 46 episodes of Silicon Valley have aired, concluding the fifth season. On April 12, 2018, it was announced that HBO had renewed the series for a sixth season.
