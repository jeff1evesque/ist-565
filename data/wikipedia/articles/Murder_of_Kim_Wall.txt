The murder of Kim Wall, also referred to in Denmark as Ubådssagen (lit. The submarine case), took place during the night of 10–11 August 2017 in Køge Bay. Wall, a Swedish freelance journalist, died onboard entrepreneur Peter Madsen's midget submarine UC3 Nautilus. On 25 April 2018, Madsen was convicted of murder and sentenced to life imprisonment by Copenhagen City Court.
