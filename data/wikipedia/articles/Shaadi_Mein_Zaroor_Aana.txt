Shaadi Mein Zaroor Aana (English: Do attend the wedding) is a 2017 Indian Hindi romantic drama film directed by Ratnaa Sinha. The film stars Rajkummar Rao as an IAS Officer and Kriti Kharbanda as a PCS Officer, who are brought together by a marriage proposal and fall in love with each other during the process but on the night of their marriage destiny takes the two of them in two different directions. The film had a worldwide release on 10 November 2017.
