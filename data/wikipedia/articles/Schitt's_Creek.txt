Schitt's Creek (stylized as Schitt$ Creek) is a Canadian television sitcom created by Eugene Levy and his son Daniel Levy, that premiered on CBC Television on January 13, 2015. The series is produced by Not a Real Company Productions. On January 12, 2015, CBC renewed the show for a second season. The second season premiered in Canada on January 12, 2016 on CBC. It was announced on February 17, 2016, that the CBC renewed the show for a third season which began broadcasting on January 11, 2017. On March 10, 2017 the show was renewed for a fourth season, which aired on January 9, 2018 on CBC. On March 6, 2018 the show was renewed for a fifth season to air in early 2019.The series has won various accolades, including an ACTRA Award and 11 Canadian Screen Awards.
