Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) (translation: All Indian Student Council) is a right-wing  all India student organisation affiliated to the Hindu nationalist Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS).  It participates in joint activities with BJP's official youth wing, Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha. It is said to be India's largest student organisation with more than three million members.
