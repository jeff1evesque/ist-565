Alan Michael Sugar, Baron Sugar (born 24 March 1947) is a British business magnate, media personality, politician and political adviser. In 2007, he sold his remaining interest in the consumer electronics company Amstrad, his largest business venture.Sugar was chairman of Tottenham Hotspur from 1991 to 2001.  Sugar appears in the BBC TV series The Apprentice, which has been broadcast annually since 2005 and is based on the US television show of the same name which was originally created by Mark Burnett starring Donald Trump.
