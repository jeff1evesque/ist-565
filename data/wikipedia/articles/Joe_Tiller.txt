Joseph Henry Tiller (December 7, 1942 – September 30, 2017) was an American football player and coach.  He served as the head football coach at the University of Wyoming from 1991 to 1996 and Purdue University from 1997 to 2008, compiling a career college football record of 126–92–1.  Tiller was known as one of the innovators of the spread offense.
