Nirav Deepak Modi (born 27 February  1971) is an Indian fugitive businessman. He is the founder of "Nirav Modi Global Diamond Jewellery House", established in 2010. He was the first Indian jeweler to have been featured on the covers of Christie’s and Sotheby’s Catalogues. The company has its headquarters in Mumbai, India.
Nirav Modi is being investigated in a $2 billion fraud case at Punjab National Bank (PNB). He absconded in early 2018, and Interpol issued a diffusion notice against Modi in February 2018. In March 2018, Modi applied for bankruptcy protection in Manhattan, New York. In June 2018, Modi was reported to be in the UK and has applied for political asylum at the Britain’s Home Office.
