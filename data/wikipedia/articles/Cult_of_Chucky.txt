Cult of Chucky is a 2017 American slasher film written and directed by Don Mancini. The seventh installment of the franchise, following the 2013 film Curse of Chucky, it stars Brad Dourif as Chucky, with a supporting cast of Fiona Dourif, Alex Vincent, Jennifer Tilly and Summer H. Howell â€“ all of whom are returning cast members from the previous six installments.
The film began production in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada in January 2017. The film premiered at the London FrightFest Film Festival on August 24, 2017, and was released with rated and unrated versions distributed by Universal Pictures Home Entertainment via Blu-ray, DVD and VOD on October 3, 2017. As of October 2017, the film has grossed $1.9 million from DVD and Blu-ray sales.
