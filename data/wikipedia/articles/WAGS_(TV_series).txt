WAGS LA is an American reality documentary television series that premiered on August 18, 2015, on E! television network. The reality show chronicles both the professional and personal lives of several WAGs (an acronym for wives and girlfriends of sportspersons).In November 2016, the network renewed the show for a third season. The first spin-off show, WAGS Miami, premiered on October 2, 2016 on the same network. The third season premiered on November 5, 2017. On May 4, 2017, it was announced that WAGS Atlanta was in development with James DuBose serving as executive producer of the series.WAGS LA and WAGS Miami were cancelled on February 1, 2018.
