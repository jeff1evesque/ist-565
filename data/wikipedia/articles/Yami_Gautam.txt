Yami Gautam (born 28 November 1988) is an Indian film actress and model who predominantly appears in Hindi films. She has also appeared in a few Tamil ,Telugu,  Kannada, Malayalam and Punjabi films. In addition to acting, she is a prominent celebrity endorser for brands and products.
In 2012, Gautam made her Bollywood debut with the film Vicky Donor. Upon release, the film was a critical and commercial success, and Gautam gained positive reviews and several nominations for her performance.Her following projects included the films Action Jackson (2014), Badlapur (2015), Sanam Re (2016) and Kaabil (2017).
