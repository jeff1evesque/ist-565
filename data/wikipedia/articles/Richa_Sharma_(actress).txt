Richa Sharma (Hindi: ऋचा शर्मा; (Aug 6, 1964– Dec 10, 1996)) also known by her married name Richa Sharma Dutt was an Indian film actress known for her works in Bollywood. Richa married actor Sanjay Dutt in New York City, United States in 1987. The couple has a daughter, Trishala Dutt. Within two years of marriage, she was diagnosed with a brain tumor. Richa died at her parents' home in New York on 10 December 1996.
