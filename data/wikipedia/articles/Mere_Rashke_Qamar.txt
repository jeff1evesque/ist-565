"Mere Rashke Qamar" (Urdu: میرے رشک قمر‬‎; Hindi: "मेरे रश्के क़मर") is a ghazal-qawwali written by lyricist ustad Qamar jalalvi and composed by Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan. It was first performed in 1988 by Khan, and popularised by him and his nephew Rahat Fateh Ali Khan several times in different concerts.
