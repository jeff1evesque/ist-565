Siddaramaiah (born 12 August 1948) is an Indian politician and was the 22nd Chief Minister of Karnataka from 2013 to 2018. He is a member of Congress working committee. He was the first Chief Minister to complete a full 5 year term in the top post in  Karnataka in 40 years.  The last chief minister to have served the state for the full term was D. Devaraj Urs from 1972 to 1977. Currently a leader of the Indian National Congress party, Siddaramaiah was a member of various Janata Parivar factions for several years. Earlier, as a Janata Dal (Secular) leader, he was Deputy Chief Minister of Karnataka on two occasions. On 13 May 2013 he was sworn in as the Chief Minister of Karnataka.
