James Michael Curtin (born 30 January 1983) is a British professional wrestler currently signed with WWE, where he is the on-screen General Manager of 205 Live, under the ring name Drake Maverick. He is known for his work with Impact Wrestling and various British and American indies under the ring name Rockstar Spud, a gimmick based on obnoxious 1980s rock stars. He has also wrestled matches as Spud, often as the underdog who defeats much larger wrestlers. He has worked for SAS Wrestling, Frontier Wrestling Alliance, and IPW:UK.
