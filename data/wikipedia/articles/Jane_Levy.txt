Jane Colburn Levy (born December 29, 1989) is an American actress. In early 2011, she received popular recognition starring as Tessa Altman, a leading character on the ABC comedy series Suburgatory (2011–14).
In 2012, Levy appeared in the films Fun Size and Nobody Walks. In 2013, Levy starred in the 2013 remake of the 1981 film The Evil Dead directed by Fede Álvarez. In 2016, she teamed up with Alvarez again and starred in the horror film Don't Breathe.
