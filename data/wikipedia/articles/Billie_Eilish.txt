Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell (born December 18, 2001), known professionally as Billie Eilish, is an American singer-songwriter. Her debut single, "Ocean Eyes", went viral and has accumulated over 87 million streams on Spotify alone as of July 2018. Her debut EP Don't Smile at Me was released in August 2017. Following the EP's success, Apple named Eilish as its newest Apple Music Up Next artist a month later in October 2017.
