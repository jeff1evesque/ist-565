Naagin (English: Female Serpent) is an Indian supernatural series produced by Ekta Kapoor and Shobha Kapoor. It is a weekend show that airs on Colors.The first season premiered on 1 November 2015. It starred Mouni Roy, Arjun Bijlani, Adaa Khan, and Sudha Chandran. The season ended on 5 June 2016 after 62 episodes. It became one of the highest rated shows on Indian television at that time. It was replaced by Kavach... Kaali Shaktiyon Se.
The second season premiered on 8 October 2016. It starred Mouni Roy, Adaa Khan, Karanvir Bohra, Sudha Chandran, Aashka Goradia, Kinshuk Mahajan. The season ended on 25 June 2017 after 75 episodes and was replaced by Chandrakanta.The third season of Naagin premiered on 2 June 2018. This season stars Surbhi Jyoti, Anita Hassanandani, Pearl V Puri in lead roles, Amrapali Gupta as main antagonist, Rakshanda Khan, Chetan Hansraj, Pavitra Punia, Mithil Jain, Charvi Saraf, Rupesh Kataria, Heli Daruwala, Simran Sachdeva, Rohit Choudhary, Aman Gandhi, Puneett Chouksey, Naveen Sharma, Ankit Mohan, Kushabh Manghani, Mahira Sharma, Adhvik Mahajan in prominent supporting roles, Karishma Tanna and Rajat Tokas in some special appearances. This season earned a 4.5 TRP rating and overall sixteen million impressions in its launch week.
