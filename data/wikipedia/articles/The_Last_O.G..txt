The Last O.G. is an American comedy television series created by Jordan Peele and John Carcieri and starring Tracy Morgan, Tiffany Haddish, Allen Maldonado, Ryan Gaul, Taylor Christian Mosby, Dante Hoagland, and Cedric the Entertainer. The series follows a recently released convict who returns to Brooklyn to find his old neighborhood has changed, and his ex-girlfriend is raising their children with a white man. The series first aired on March 31, 2018, on TBS. On April 23, 2018, it was announced that the series had been renewed for a second season ("O.G." stands for original gangster).
