The 2018–19 UEFA Champions League qualifying phase and play-off round began on 26 June and ended on 29 August 2018.A total of 53 teams competed in the qualifying system of the 2018–19 UEFA Champions League, which includes the qualifying phase and the play-off round, with 43 teams in Champions Path and 10 teams in League Path. The six winners in the play-off round (four from Champions Path, two from League Path) advanced to the group stage, to join the 26 teams that enter in the group stage.Times are CEST (UTC+2), as listed by UEFA (local times, if different, are in parentheses).
