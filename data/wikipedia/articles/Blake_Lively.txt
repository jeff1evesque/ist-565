Blake Ellender Lively (née Brown; August 25, 1987) is an American actress. 
Lively is known for starring as Serena van der Woodsen in the CW drama television series Gossip Girl (2007–2012). She also starred in the films The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants (2005), Accepted (2006), The Private Lives of Pippa Lee (2009), The Town (2010), Green Lantern (2011), Savages (2012), The Age of Adaline (2015), The Shallows (2016), Café Society (2016), and All I See Is You (2017).She has been married to actor Ryan Reynolds since 2012, with whom she has two children.
