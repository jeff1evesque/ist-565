The Visit is a 2015 American found footage  horror film written, co-produced and directed by M. Night Shyamalan, and starring Olivia DeJonge, Ed Oxenbould, Deanna Dunagan, Peter McRobbie, and Kathryn Hahn.
The film was released in North America on September 11, 2015 by Universal Pictures. The film received overall mixed to positive reviews, and some viewed it as a comeback for Shyamalan. It was a box office success, grossing $98.5 million worldwide against its $5 million budget.
