Matthew Charles Czuchry (; born May 20, 1977) is an American actor. He is known for roles such as that of Logan Huntzberger on The WB television series Gilmore Girls (2005–07), Cary Agos on the CBS television drama The Good Wife (2009–16), and currently as Dr. Conrad Hawkins, the title character, on the Fox medical drama series The Resident (2018–present).
