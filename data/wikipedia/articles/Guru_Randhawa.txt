Guru Randhawa is an Indian singer and songwriter from Punjab, India. Guru Randhawa is known for his composed tracks like “Lahore”, “Patola”, “High Rated Gabru”, “Suit”, "Ban Ja Rani", "Made in India", and “Ishare Tere”.  He sang in the 2018 Indian Premier League opening ceremony. He  made his Bollywood singing debut in Hindi Medium. He recreated his song Suit for film Hindi Medium, song Tu Meri Rani for film Tumhari Sulu, Patola for the film Blackmail, and High Rated Gabru for Nawabzaade. He also sang for films Simran, Dil Juunglee, Sonu ke Titu Ki Sweety. In 2018, he became a part of Salman Khan's Dabangg reloaded tour. His song Lahore is the first Punjabi song to cross 500 million views on YouTube.
