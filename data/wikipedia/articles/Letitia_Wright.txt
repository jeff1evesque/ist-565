Letitia Michelle Wright (born 31 October 1993) is a  Guyanese-born British actress. Beginning in 2011, she has played in roles in several British TV series, including Top Boy, Coming Up, Chasing Shadows, Humans, the Doctor Who episode "Face the Raven" and the Black Mirror episode "Black Museum", the latter for which she received a Primetime Emmy Award nomination.
The British Academy of Film and Television Arts (BAFTA) named Wright among the 2015 group of BAFTA Breakthrough Brits for her role in the award winning film Urban Hymn. Wright is best known for playing the character Shuri in the Marvel Cinematic Universe films Black Panther and Avengers: Infinity War (both released in 2018).
