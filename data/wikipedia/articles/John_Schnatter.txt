John H. Schnatter (born November 22, 1961), nicknamed commercially as Papa John, is an American entrepreneur who founded Papa John's Pizza. Schnatter stepped down as CEO on January 1, 2018, after comments he made criticizing National Football League commissioner Roger Goodell for not doing anything about national anthem protests. He was succeeded by President and COO Steve Ritchie. He stepped down as chairman of the board on July 11, 2018 following his use of a racial slur during a conference call with a marketing agency.
