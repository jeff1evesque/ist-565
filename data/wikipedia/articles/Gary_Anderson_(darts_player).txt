Gary Anderson (born 22 December 1970) is a Scottish professional darts player from Musselburgh, currently playing in the Professional Darts Corporation, and a former BDO and WDF world number one. He is a two-time PDC World Champion, having won the title in 2015 and 2016. He is nicknamed The Flying Scotsman.
Anderson is managed by Tommy Gilmour MBE and is part of the Dunvegan Enterprises Team alongside Michael Smith, Ian White, Chris Dobey, Chris Quantock and Adam Hunt.
Anderson is renowned for his heavy scoring in the game and having one of the smoothest throws.
