Naga Chaitanya Akkineni (born 23 November 1986) is an Indian film actor who works in Telugu cinema. He debuted in the 2009 film Josh, directed by newcomer Vasu Varma and produced by Dil Raju. Prior to the release of his first film, he signed his second film with Indira Productions, which was to be directed by Gautham Menon. Ye Maaya Chesave co-starring Samantha Akkineni released in 2010 with comparisons drawn to Chaitanya's father Akkineni Nagarjuna's romantic film Geethanjali. his next film was
Sukumar's 100% Love, co-starring Tamannaah. The movie was soon followed by Dhada, co-starring Kajal Aggarwal and Bejawada. Chaitanya also appeared in the movie Autonagar Surya. In 2016, he starred in two  films, Premam, co-starring Shruti Haasan and Sahasam Swasaga Sagipo, which earned him critical acclaim. Subsequently, in 2017, he starred in Rarandoi Veduka Chudham, which became his career's highest grosser till date.
