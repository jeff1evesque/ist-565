Raaz Reboot is a 2016 Indian horror thriller film written and directed by Vikram Bhatt. It features Emraan Hashmi , Kriti Kharbanda and Gaurav Arora in the lead roles. The film is the fourth instalment in the horror film series, Raaz.Raaz: Reboot was released worldwide on 16 September 2016. Principal photography commenced in January 2016 in Romania.
