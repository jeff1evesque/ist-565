Macaco Tião ("Tião the Monkey") (January 16, 1963 in Rio de Janeiro – December 23, 1996 in Rio de Janeiro) was a chimpanzee of the Rio Janeiro Zoo who was very popular with children and visitors.His name ("Tião") is in memory of Rio de Janeiro's patron saint, São Sebastião. He was 1.52 metres (5 ft 0 in) tall and weighed 70 kilograms (150 lb).
