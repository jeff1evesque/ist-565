Jessica Yu Li Henwick (玉李; born 30 August 1992) is an English actress. She was the first actress of East Asian descent to play the lead role in a British television series, having starred in the children's show Spirit Warriors (2010). She is also known for her roles as Nymeria Sand in HBO series Game of Thrones (2015-17),  X-wing pilot Jessika Pava in Star Wars: The Force Awakens (2015), and the character Colleen Wing in Iron Fist (2017), The Defenders (2017) and the second season of Luke Cage (2018).
