Leslie Edwin Miles (born November 10, 1953) is an American football coach and former player and head coach. From 2005 to 2016  he was at LSU. Before his tenure at LSU, Miles was the coach for the Oklahoma State Cowboys from 2001 to 2004. Miles is nicknamed "The Hat" for his signature white cap, as well as "The Mad Hatter" for his eccentricities and play-calling habits. Prior to being a head coach, he was an assistant coach at Oklahoma State as well as at the University of Michigan, the University of Colorado at Boulder, and with the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League (NFL).
