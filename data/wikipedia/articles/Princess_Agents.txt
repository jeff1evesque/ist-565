Princess Agents (Chinese: 特工皇妃楚乔传) is a 2017 Chinese television series based on the novel 11 Chu Te Gong Huang Fei (11处特工皇妃) written by Xiao Xiang Dong Er (潇湘冬儿). It stars Zhao Liying, Lin Gengxin, Shawn Dou and Li Qin.
The series aired on Hunan TV from 5 June to 1 August 2017.Princess Agents has received overwhelming success both domestically and internationally, and is currently the one of the most watched Chinese drama of all time, with more than 40 billion views on Chinese streaming sites.
