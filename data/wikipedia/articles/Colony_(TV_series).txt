Colony is an American science fiction drama television series created by Carlton Cuse and Ryan J. Condal, starring Josh Holloway and Sarah Wayne Callies. A 10-episode first season premiered with an online preview release of the first episode on USA Network's website on December 15, 2015, following the launch of a game-like website to promote the show. The series had its broadcast premiere on USA Network on January 14, 2016.  In April 2017, Colony was renewed for a third season which premiered on May 2, 2018. On July 21, 2018, USA announced they had cancelled the series after three seasons.
