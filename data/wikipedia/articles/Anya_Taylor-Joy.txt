Anya Taylor-Joy (born 16 April 1996) is an American-born British-Argentinian actress. She is best known for her scream queen roles as Thomasin in the horror film The Witch (2015), as Casey Cooke in the horror-thriller film Split (2016) and as Lily in the black comedy thriller film Thoroughbreds (2018). Taylor-Joy was nominated for the BAFTA Rising Star Award in 2017.
