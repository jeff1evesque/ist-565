Kaabil (English: Capable) is a 2017 Indian Hindi-language crime thriller film directed by Sanjay Gupta, written by Vijay Kumar Mishra, produced by Rakesh Roshan under his banner FilmKraft Productions. It features a love affair between two blind people, played by Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam. Music is composed by Rajesh Roshan. Principal photography of the film began on 30 March 2016. The film was released on 25 January 2017. Kaabil was released in Tamil and Telugu under the title Balam (Strength) Roshan received the Nomination of Filmfare Award for Best Actor for his performance at the 63rd Filmfare Awards.
