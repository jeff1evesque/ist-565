Enzo Alan Zidane Fernández (born 24 March 1995), simply known as Enzo, is a French footballer who plays for Spanish club CF Rayo Majadahonda on loan from Swiss club FC Lausanne-Sport as a midfielder.
Enzo is the eldest son of retired French footballer Zinedine Zidane and of Véronique Fernández. He attained Spanish citizenship in 2006.
