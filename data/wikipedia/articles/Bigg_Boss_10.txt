Bigg Boss 10 is the tenth season of the Indian reality TV series Bigg Boss. It began airing on 16 October 2016 on Colors. The show is also available after the original telecast on Viacom 18's digital platform – Voot. A new element called ‘Unseen-Undekha’ was introduced by way of unseen footage uploaded on Voot.This footage showed parts of the day that weren’t included in the episode, from ‘wake-up call’ to ‘lights out’.
The series was won by commoner Manveer Gurjar while reality star Gurbani Judge finished runner-up.
