Shikkari Shambhu is a 2018 Indian Malayalam-language comedy drama thriller film directed by Sugeeth and written by Nishad Koya. The film features Kunchacko Boban, Sshivada, Vishnu Unnikrishnan, Hareesh Kanaran and Alphy Panjikaran in lead roles. Produced and distributed by S. K. Lawrence through Angel Maria Cinemas. The film was released in India on 27 January 2018, and received positive response from audience.The film was a Super Hit at the box office.
