Tin Star is a British-Canadian drama series created by Rowan Joffe that debuted all episodes on Sky Atlantic on 7 September 2017 and was released on Amazon Prime in the USA on 29 September 2017. The ten-part series stars Tim Roth, Genevieve O'Reilly, Abigail Lawrie, Oliver Coopersmith and Christina Hendricks. It has been renewed for a second season.
