Leslie Roy Moonves (; born October 6, 1949) is an American media executive. He currently serves as Chairman of the Board, President, and Chief Executive Officer of CBS Corporation.Moonves served as Co-President and Co-Chief Operating Officer of Viacom, Inc., the predecessor to CBS Corporation, from 2004 until the company split on December 31, 2005. Prior to that, he had had a series of executive positions at CBS since July 1995. He has been on the Board of Directors at ZeniMax Media since 1999. He became Chairman of CBS in February 2016.
