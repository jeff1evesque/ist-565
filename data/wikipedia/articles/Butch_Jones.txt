Lyle Allen "Butch" Jones Jr. (born January 17, 1968) is an American football analyst. He was the head coach at the University of Tennessee from 2013 to 2017, University of Cincinnati from 2010 to 2012 and Central Michigan University (CMU) from 2007 to 2009. A Michigan native, he played college football at Ferris State University as a running back and wide receiver.
