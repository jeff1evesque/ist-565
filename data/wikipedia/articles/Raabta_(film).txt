Raabta (English: Connection) is an Indian epic romantic adventure film directed and produced by Dinesh Vijan co-produced by Homi Adajania and Bhushan Kumar. The film stars Sushant Singh Rajput, Kriti Sanon and Jim Sarbh in the lead roles. It was released worldwide on 9 June 2017. The storyline of the film is notably similar to that of the 2009 Telugu film, Magadheera.
