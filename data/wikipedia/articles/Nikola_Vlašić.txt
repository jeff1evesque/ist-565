Nikola Vlašić (Croatian pronunciation: [nǐkola ʋlâʃitɕ]; born 4 October 1997) is a Croatian professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Russian Premier League club PFC CSKA Moscow on loan from Everton and the Croatia national team.
In 2014, Vlašić made list of the world's top 40 footballers born in 1997, compiled by The Guardian.
