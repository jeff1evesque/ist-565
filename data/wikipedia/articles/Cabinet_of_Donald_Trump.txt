This article lists the members of President Donald Trump's Cabinet. Trump assumed office on January 20, 2017.
The President of the United States has the authority to nominate members of their Cabinet to the United States Senate for confirmation under Appointments Clause of the United States Constitution. Before confirmation and during congressional hearings, a high-level career member of an executive department heads this pre-confirmed cabinet on an acting basis. The Cabinet's creation is part of the transition of power following the 2016 United States presidential election.
This page documents the confirmation process for any successful or unsuccessful Cabinet nominees of Donald Trump's administration. They are listed in order of creation of the Cabinet position (also used as the basis for the United States presidential line of succession).
