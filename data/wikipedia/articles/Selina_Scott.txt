Selina Mary Scott (born 13 May 1951) is a British television presenter who amongst many landmark ‘TV firsts’ launched breakfast TV in the UK before crossing the Atlantic to join West 57th, a seminal, US prime time current affairs show broadcast from New York. Scott continues to write, and run her luxury lifestyle brand, Naturally Selina Scott.
Scott’s most recent television appearance was on the BBC’s (series 3) The Real Marigold Hotel, shot in Rajasthan, which she had always wanted to visit as her great-great-grandfather (a soldier surgeon) survived the murderous siege of Lucknow.
