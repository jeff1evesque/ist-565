Ishita Raj Sharma (born 8 February 1990) is an Indian film actress and model, best known for her work in Pyaar Ka Punchnama and Sonu Ke Titu Ki Sweety.Ishita completed her B. Com. from Gargi College, New Delhi and then moved to England to purse a major in business.== Career = Ishita acted in Pyaar Ka Punchnama (2011), produced by Wide Frame Pictures in which she played one of the three girls named Charu. The film became a sleeper hit with newcomers in the lead. It has now achieved a cult status among the youth. Ishita Sharma also worked in Meeruthiya Gangsters in 2015.Ishita was in Luv Ranjan’s Pyaar Ka Panchnama 2, in which she was paired with Omkar Kapoor. In 2018, she featured in Sonu Ke Titu Ki Sweety, which crossed more than ₹ 100 crore in India.
