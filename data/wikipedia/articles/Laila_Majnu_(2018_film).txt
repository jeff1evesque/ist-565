Laila Majnu is an upcoming Indian romance film starring Avinash Tiwari and Tripti Dimri. It is presented by Imtiaz Ali and co-produced by Ekta Kapoor, Shobha Kapoor and Preety Ali. The film is directed by Sajid Ali.The trailer was released on 7 August 2018 and the film is set to release on 7 September 2018.
