Tyron Lakent Woodley (born April 7, 1982) is an American professional mixed martial artist, broadcast analyst and actor who is currently signed with the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). He competes in the UFC's welterweight division, where he is the reigning champion. A professional since 2009, Woodley also fought in Strikeforce. As of 26 July 2018, , he is #7 in official UFC pound-for-pound rankings.
