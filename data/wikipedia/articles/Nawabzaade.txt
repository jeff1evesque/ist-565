Nawabzaade (transl. Prince's son; Hindi pronunciation: [nəʋaːbzaːd̪eː]) is a 2018 Bollywood romantic comedy film starring Raghav Juyal, Punit Pathak and Dharmesh Yelande in leading roles and also features Varun Dhawan, Shraddha Kapoor, Athiya Shetty and Badshah. Produced by Lizelle D'Souza under R.D.Entertainment and directed by Jayesh Pradhan, the film was released on 27 July 2018. The first official song. "High Rated Gabru", was released on 28 June 2018, followed by "Tere naal Nachna" on 5 July.
