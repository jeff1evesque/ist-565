Bigg Boss Tamil 1 is the first season of the reality TV programme Bigg Boss Tamil. The first season aired on Star Vijay from 25 June to 30 September 2017, and aired from 9:00 to 10:30 pm on weekdays and 8:30 to 10:00 pm on weekends.A lavish house set was constructed at EVP theme park, Chennai. The season's presenter was Kamal Haasan.
