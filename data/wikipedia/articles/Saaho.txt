Saaho is an upcoming Indian film written and directed by Sujeeth and produced by UV Creations and T-Series.. The film stars Prabhas, Shraddha Kapoor, Neil Nitin Mukesh and Evelyn Sharma, and is being shot simultaneously in Telugu, Tamil and Hindi. Filming began in June 2017. Saaho's first look poster released on 23 October 2017, on the occasion of Prabhas' birthday. A second teaser will be released on 23 October 2018, once again on the occasion of Prabhas' birthday. It is the second most expensive Indian film made to date.
