Nora Fatehi (born 6 February 1992 ) is a Morrocan-Canadian dancer, model, and actress, of Indian and Moroccan origin. She made her film debut in the Bollywood film Roar: Tigers of the Sundarbans.  She gained popularity in Telugu cinema by doing item numbers in films like Temper, Baahubali and Kick 2. She has also starred in a Malayalam film Double Barrel. She was a contestant on Bigg Boss 9 and got evicted on day 84.
She starred in Satyamev Jayate, released on August 15, in which she was seen in the recreated version of the song "Dilbar" which crossed 20 million views in the first 24 hours of its release, making it the first Hindi song to have garnered such numbers in India.
