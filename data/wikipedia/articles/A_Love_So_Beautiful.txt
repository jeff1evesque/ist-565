A Love So Beautiful (Chinese: 致我们单纯的小美好; pinyin: Zhì Wǒ Mén Dān Chún Dí Xiǎo Měi Hǎo) is a Chinese web series starring Hu Yitian and Shen Yue, based on the novel To Our Pure Little Beauty by Zhao Gangan. It aired on Tencent Video from November 9 until December 7, 2017.The series was popular during its run, and received positive reviews for its characterization and story. It surpassed more than 5.5 billion views on its online platform.
