Vijay Antony is an Indian music composer, playback singer, actor, film editor, lyricist, sound engineer and producer working in the Tamil, Telugu and Kannada film industries. He made his debut as music director in 2005 and as a lead actor in 2012. He is the first Indian to win the 2009 Cannes Golden Lion for the "Nakka Mukka" advertising film in the Best Music category. The song propelled his fame and was played at the 2011 Cricket World Cup.
