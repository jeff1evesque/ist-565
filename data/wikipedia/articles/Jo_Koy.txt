Joseph Glenn Herbert (born June 2, 1971), known professionally as Jo Koy, is a Filipino-American stand-up comic. Koy is currently touring as a headliner in clubs and theaters across the country on his new tour called Break The Mold.   He was a frequent  panelist on E!'s late night show Chelsea Lately. Jo has gained a large following of fans lately from his semi-regular appearances on The Adam Carolla Show, where he does numerous impressions from P.F. Chang's greeter to Bung Lu Su.
