Akiko Matsuura (Also known by the name Akiko "Keex" Matsuura) is a drummer and vocalist who is a member of the English bands Pre and Comanechi along with guitarist, Simon Petrovich.. Originally from Osaka, Japan, she also plays drums with The Big Pink and has a side project called Sperm Javelin. As of 2013, Comanechi is on permanent hiatus, and Matsuura has also left drumming duties in the Big Pink.
