The Appearance of Christ Before the People or  The Apparition of the Messiah (Russian: Явление Христа народу) is an oil painting on canvas, measuring  540 cm × 750 cm, by the Russian painter Alexander Andreyevich Ivanov (1806–1858).The painting has been called  his magnum opus and took 20 years to complete (1837–1857). The narrative of the painting is based on the third chapter of the Gospel of Matthew, also described in the first chapter of the Gospel according to John.
