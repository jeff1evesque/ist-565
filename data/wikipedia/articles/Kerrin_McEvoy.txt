Kerrin McEvoy (born 28 October 1980) is an Australian jockey who is best known for riding Brew to victory in the 2000 Melbourne Cup. In Europe, McEvoy rode several big winners for Godolphin including Rule of Law in the St Leger Stakes at Doncaster in 2004 and Ibn Khaldun in the Racing Post Trophy, also at Doncaster in 2007. In 2016, McEvoy won his second Melbourne Cup, riding Almandin to victory.
