The Amazing Race 30 is the thirtieth installment of the reality television show The Amazing Race. It features eleven teams of two in a race around the world for a US$1 million grand prize. The season premiered on January 3, 2018, airing in CBS's Wednesday 8:00 pm EST time slot for a month before moving to the 9:00 pm slot from February 7 due to the premiere of Celebrity Big Brother. The 2-hour season finale aired on February 21, 2018. 
Dating reality stars Cody Nickson and Jessica Graf were the winners of this season of the Race, making them the first team previously cast on Big Brother to win The Amazing Race.
