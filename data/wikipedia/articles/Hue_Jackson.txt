Hue Jackson (born October 22, 1965) is an American football coach who is the current head coach of the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League (NFL). Previously, he served as the offensive coordinator and head coach of the Oakland Raiders and offensive coordinator of the Cincinnati Bengals. Before joining Oakland, Jackson served as offensive assistant coach for several NFL teams, most notably as the offensive coordinator for the Washington Redskins under Steve Spurrier and the Atlanta Falcons under Bobby Petrino.
