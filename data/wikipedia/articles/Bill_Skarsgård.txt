Bill Istvan Günther Skarsgård (Swedish pronunciation: [ˈbɪl ˈʂkɑːʂgoːɖ] ( listen); born 9 August 1990) is a Swedish actor. He is best known for starring in The Divergent Series: Allegiant, Atomic Blonde, the Netflix original series Hemlock Grove, and for playing Pennywise the Dancing Clown in the 2017 adaptation of It.
