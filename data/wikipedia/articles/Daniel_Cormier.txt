Daniel Cormier (born March 20, 1979) is an American mixed martial artist and former Olympic wrestler. He is currently signed to Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), where he is the champion of both the light heavyweight and heavyweight divisions, and is ranked number 1 Pound-for-Pound (P4P) fighter in the world. Cormier is the second of two fighters in UFC history to hold titles in two weight classes simultaneously. Prior to the UFC, Cormier was Strikeforce Heavyweight Grand Prix Champion and King of the Cage Heavyweight Champion. He holds the distinction of having won a world championship belt in every promotion in which he has fought. Cormier is considered to be among the greatest mixed martial artists of all time.
