Maanayata Dutt (born Dilnawaz Shaikh) is an Indian entrepreneur and the current C.E.O. of Sanjay Dutt Productions. She is the wife of Bollywood actor Sanjay Dutt. She is best known for her item number in Prakash Jha's 2003-hit Gangaajal. She also acted in Mere Baap Pehle Aap (translation: My father, You first), a 2008 Indian Hindi-language comedy-drama film directed by Priyadarshan.
