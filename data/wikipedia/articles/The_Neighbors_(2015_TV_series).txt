The Neighbors is a sitcom created, written, directed, produced by, and starring Tommy Wiseau. In Wiseau's first such creative excursion since his 2003 cult film The Room, it is set in an apartment building and revolves around the lives of the various tenants and their interactions with the "manager", Charlie, played by Wiseau. Four episodes were released on Hulu on March 14, 2015. Two additional episodes were released on May 26, 2015.
