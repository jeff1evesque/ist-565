Jordi Alba Ramos (Catalan: [ˈʒɔrði ˈaɫβə ˈramos], Spanish: [ˈɟʝoɾði ˈalβa ˈramos]; born 21 March 1989) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for Barcelona and the Spain national team.  Mainly a left-back, he can also operate as a left winger.He started his career at Barcelona, but was released for being deemed too small. After joining neighbouring Cornellà, he finished his development at Valencia. In 2012, he returned to Barcelona.
Alba represented Spain at two World Cups and as many European Championships, having first appeared for the team in 2011. He won the Euro 2012 tournament.
