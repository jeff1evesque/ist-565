Guardian: The Lonely and Great God (Hangul: 쓸쓸하고 찬란하神 – 도깨비; RR: Sseulsseulhago Chanlanhasin – Dokkaebi; lit. The Lonely and Great God – Goblin) is a South Korean television series starring Gong Yoo in the title role with Kim Go-eun, Lee Dong-wook, Yoo In-na, and Yook Sung-jae. It aired on cable network tvN starting December 2, 2016 until January 21, 2017.Guardian's final episode recorded an 18.68% nationwide audience share, making it the second highest rated drama in Korean cable television history, behind Reply 1988. It received critical acclaim and became a cultural phenomenon in South Korea.
