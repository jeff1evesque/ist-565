Matthew Edward Patricia (born September 13, 1974) is an American football coach who is the head coach of the Detroit Lions of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), where he was a four-year letterman as an offensive lineman. From 2004 to 2017, Patricia worked for the New England Patriots, notably as their defensive coordinator from 2012 to 2017.
