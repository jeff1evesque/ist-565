Gustaf Caspar Orm Skarsgård (Swedish: [²ɡɵstav ˈskɑːʂɡoːɖ] ( listen); born 12 November 1980) is a Swedish actor. He is best known outside Scandinavia for his role as Floki in the History Channel series Vikings as well as for his roles in the films Evil (2003), The Way Back (2010) and Kon-Tiki (2012). He also played Karl Strand in the second season of the HBO series Westworld.
