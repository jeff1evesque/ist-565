Cody Ray Allen Garbrandt (born July 7, 1991) is an American professional mixed martial artist signed to the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). He is a former Pinnacle FC fighter and the former UFC Bantamweight Champion after becoming only the second fighter to defeat Dominick Cruz at UFC 207, handing him his first defeat in almost a decade. 
As of July 26, 2018, he is the #1 ranked UFC Bantamweight contender. He is #11 in the UFC pound-for-pound rankings.
