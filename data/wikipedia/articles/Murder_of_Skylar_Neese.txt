Neese's remains were found on January 16, 2013, in Wayne Township, Greene County, Pennsylvania.Neese was murdered by two of her high school friends, Rachel Shoaf and Shelia Eddy. In December 2012, Shoaf told authorities that she and Eddy planned and carried out Neese's murder. Shoaf pleaded guilty to second-degree murder on May 1, 2013. Neese's disappearance led to new West Virginia legislation that made changes to the AMBER Alert missing child alert system.
