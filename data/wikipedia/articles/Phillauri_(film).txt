Phillauri is a 2017 Indian Hindi fantasy comedy film directed by Anshai Lal and produced by Fox Star Studios with Anushka Sharma and her brother Karnesh Sharma under their banner of Clean Slate Films. Director Navdeep Singh and writer Sudip Sharma are associated with the film  as Creative Producers. The film stars Anushka Sharma, Diljit Dosanjh, Suraj Sharma & Mehreen Pirzada in the lead roles. The film's first official poster was released on February 3, 2017. The film was released on March 24, 2017.
