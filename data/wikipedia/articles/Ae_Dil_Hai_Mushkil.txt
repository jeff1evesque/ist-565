Ae Dil Hai Mushkil (lit. O heart, it is difficult) is a 2016 Indian romantic drama film written and directed by Karan Johar. It features Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma, and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan in lead roles and tells the story of unrequited love. It was released on 28 October 2016 during the Diwali festival. 
The film earned ₹2.37 billion (US$34 million) worldwide against a budget of ₹700 million (US$10 million). At the 62nd Filmfare Awards, Ae Dil Hai Mushkil received nine nominations, including Best Director for Johar, and won four awards for its soundtrack.
