Hichki (transl. Hiccup; Hindi pronunciation: [ɦɪtʃkiː]) is a 2018 Indian Hindi comedy-drama film, directed by Sidharth P Malhotra and produced by Maneesh Sharma under the banner Yash Raj Films. The film is an adaptation of Brad Cohen's autobiography Front of the Class: How Tourette Syndrome Made Me the Teacher I Never Had, of which Yash Raj Films acquired the rights.Hichki stars Rani Mukerji in the lead role of an aspiring teacher having Tourette syndrome, who must prove herself by educating a group of underprivileged students. The film's trailer was launched on December 19, 2017, and it was theatrically released on 23 March 2018 to generally positive reviews from critics. Hichki was also screened at the Shanghai International Film Festival on June 16, 2018.
