Kim Yo-jong (Korean: 김여정, born 26 September 1987) is the younger sister of North Korea's leader Kim Jong-un. She was also the youngest daughter of late leader Kim Jong-il and granddaughter of late founder Kim Il-sung. She is an alternate member of the Politburo (Political Bureau) and the Vice Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Workers' Party of Korea (WPK).
