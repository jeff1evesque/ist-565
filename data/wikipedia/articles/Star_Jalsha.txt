Star Jalsha (স্টার জলসা) is a Bengali language General Entertainment (GEC)  Television Channel in India. STAR India network. Its programming includes a mix of family dramas, comedies, reality shows, shows on crime and telefilms. The channel also airs the latest Bengali films. The channel launched its HD feed on 14 April 2016.
The channel is distributed worldwide by Fox International Channels, a subsidiary of 21st Century Fox.
