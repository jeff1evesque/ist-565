Geetha Govindam is an Indian Telugu romcom film directed by Parasuram, produced by Bunny Vas under the banner of GA2 Pictures. It stars Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna in the lead roles, while Subbaraju, Rahul Ramakrishna, Vennela Kishore, Naga Babu,  and Mouryani appear in supporting roles. It was released on 15 August 2018 in India and on 14 August 2018 in the US. The film received extremely positive reviews from critics and became a blockbuster at the box office.
