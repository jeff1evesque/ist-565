Hasan Minhaj (; born September 23, 1985) is an American comedian and actor. He had been a senior correspondent on The Daily Show, which he joined in 2014. Minhaj was the featured speaker at the 2017 White House Correspondents' Dinner. His first stand-up comedy special, Homecoming King, debuted on Netflix on May 23, 2017, and received positive reviews from critics and a Peabody Award in 2018. Minhaj left Daily Show in August 2018 to shift focus to host a weekly comedy show, Patriot Act with Hasan Minhaj on Netflix starting October 28, 2018. It will explore the modern cultural and political landscape with depth and sincerity.
