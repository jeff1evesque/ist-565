You're the Worst is an American single-camera comedy-drama television series created by Stephen Falk. Originally broadcast by FX, the series currently airs on sister channel FXX. The series follows Jimmy (Chris Geere), a self-involved writer, and Gretchen (Aya Cash), a self-destructive Los Angeles PR executive, as these two attempt a relationship, along with their two friends Edgar (Desmin Borges) and Lindsay (Kether Donohue). The series premiered on July 17, 2014.On September 30, 2014, FX renewed the show for a thirteen-episode second season, which premiered September 9, 2015 on FXX. The second season focused on clinical depression and its effect on modern relationships. In September 2016, FX renewed the series for a fourth season, which premiered on September 6, 2017. In November 2017, FX renewed the series for a fifth and final season to premiere in January 2019.
