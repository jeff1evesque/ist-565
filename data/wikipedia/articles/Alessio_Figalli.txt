Alessio Figalli (Italian: [aˈlesːjo fiˈɡalːi]; born 2 April 1984) is an Italian mathematician working primarily on calculus of variations and partial differential equations.
He was awarded the Prix and Cours Peccot in 2012, the EMS Prize in 2012, the Stampacchia Medal in 2015, the Feltrinelli Prize in 2017 and the Fields Medal in 2018. He was an invited speaker at the International Congress of Mathematicians 2014.
In 2016 he was awarded an ERC grant, and in 2018 he received the Fields Medal.
