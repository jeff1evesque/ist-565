Radzi Chinyanganya (born 12 September 1986) is a British presenter of the BBC children's TV programme Blue Peter, beginning in 2013, and a co-presenter for the ITV gameshow Cannonball since 2017. Previously he presented Wild on CBBC and was also the host of Match of the Day Kickabout from 2013 until 2014, when Ben Shires took over.
