Cameron Riley Monaghan (born August 16, 1993) is an American actor and model. Beginning with his professional career as a child model at the age of three, and child actor at the age of seven, Monaghan is best known for his role as Ian Gallagher on the Showtime comedy-drama series Shameless. He has also appeared as twin brothers Jerome and Jeremiah Valeska, characters similar to The Joker in the Fox crime series Gotham.
