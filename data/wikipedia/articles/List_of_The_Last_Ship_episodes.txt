The Last Ship is an American post-apocalyptic drama television series based on a novel of the same name by William Brinkley. In May 2013, the cable network TNT placed a 10-episode order for the series. The series premiered on June 22, 2014, at 9:00 p.m. EDT.As of  October 8, 2017, 46 episodes of The Last Ship have aired, concluding the fourth season. In September 2016, TNT renewed the series for a 10-episode fifth and final season, which is scheduled to premiere on September 9, 2018.
