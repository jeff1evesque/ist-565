Ittefaq (English: Coincidence) is a 2017 Indian mystery-thriller film, directed by Abhay Chopra and produced by Red Chillies Entertainment in association with BR Studios & Dharma Productions. The film is inspired from the 1969 film by the same name, directed by Yash Chopra. It stars Akshaye Khanna, Sidharth Malhotra and Sonakshi Sinha in lead roles. The film has a Rashomon effect storytelling. The film was released on 3 November 2017.
