The Izumo-class helicopter destroyer (いずも型護衛艦, Izumo-gata-goei-kan) or 22DDH is a helicopter carrier class constructed for the Japan Maritime Self-Defense Force (JMSDF).  The ships of this class are currently the largest surface combatants of the JMSDF, taking over the mantle previously  held by the Hyūga-class helicopter destroyers. The lead ship was officially unveiled at Yokohama on 6 August 2013.
