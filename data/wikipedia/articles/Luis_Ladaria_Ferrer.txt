Luis Francisco Ladaria Ferrer  (born 19 April 1944) is a Spanish Jesuit, theologian and a cardinal of the Roman Catholic Church. After a thirty-year career teaching theology, he joined the Roman Curia in 2004 as Secretary-General of the International Theological Commission. An archbishop since 2008, he became Prefect of the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith (CDF) in 2017.
Pope Francis made him a cardinal on 28 June 2018.
