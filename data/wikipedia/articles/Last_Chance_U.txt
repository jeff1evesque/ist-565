Last Chance U is an American documentary television series that is produced and premiered by Netflix. The seven-episode first season, directed by Greg Whiteley, explores the football program at East Mississippi Community College, which features several collegiate athletes that have been had trouble in their lives and struggled with finding structure. The players are then required to perform at the junior college (JUCO) level, under the stewardship of coach Buddy Stephens, in order to prove themselves and return to Division I.The series' second season returned to Mississippi, but transitioned to Independence Community College in Kansas for the show's third season, which premiered on July 20, 2018.
