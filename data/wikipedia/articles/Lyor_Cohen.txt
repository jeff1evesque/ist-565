Lyor Cohen (Hebrew: ליאור כהן; born October 3, 1959) is an American music industry executive. 
Cohen has been actively involved in hip-hop at various record labels for more than 30 years. He started by managing rappers for Rush Productions, then led Def Jam.After Def Jam, Cohen took on a leadership role at Warner Music Group. In September 2012, Cohen put in his resignation at Warner and started his own independent label, 300 Entertainment.
On September 28, 2016, Cohen was named YouTube's Global Head of Music.
