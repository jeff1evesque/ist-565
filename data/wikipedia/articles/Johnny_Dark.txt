Johnny Dark (born John J. Koletes, December 29, 1940) is an American comedian and comic actor, active on television since the 1970s.  He is most recently known for his recurring appearances on Late Show with David Letterman.In addition to his Late Show appearances, his appearances as a comedian include Late Night with David Letterman, The Tonight Show Starring Johnny Carson, Donny and Marie, and The Alan Hamel Show.  He has also done stand-up comedy in Atlantic City and, as an actor, appeared in several films.
