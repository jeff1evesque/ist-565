Kahaani 2: Durga Rani Singh (or simply Kahaani 2, English: Story 2) is a 2016 Indian Hindi-language mystery thriller film directed by Sujoy Ghosh and produced by Ghosh and Jayantilal Gada. A sequel to the 2012 film Kahaani, the film features Arjun Rampal and Vidya Balan in lead roles, and Jugal Hansraj and Kharaj Mukherjee in supporting roles. Kahaani 2 is the story of Durga Rani Singh, who is charged with kidnapping and murder.
Principal photography took place from March 2016 to May 2016. The film was released on 2 December 2016. It was declared tax-free in Uttar Pradesh on 15 December 2016. At the 62nd Filmfare Awards, Balan received a Best Actress nomination for her performance.
