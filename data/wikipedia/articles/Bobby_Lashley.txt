Robert Franklin Lashley (born July 16, 1976) is an American professional wrestler and mixed martial artist currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the Raw
 brand.
During Lashley's initial tenure with WWE, he was a two-time ECW World Champion and one-time United States Champion. Lashley is also known for his time in Impact Wrestling, where he was a four-time Impact World Heavyweight Champion, a one-time X Division Champion, and one-time and final King of the Mountain Champion. Between WWE and Impact, Lashley has won a total of 9 championships, and headlined multiple pay-per-view events.
