Tempted (Hangul: 위대한 유혹자; RR: Widaehan Yuhokja; lit. The Great Seducer) is a 2018 South Korean television series starring Woo Do-hwan, Park Soo-yeong, Moon Ga-young and Kim Min-jae. It is loosely based on the French novel titled Les Liaisons dangereuses by Pierre Choderlos de Laclos. It aired on MBC from March 12, 2018 to May 1, 2018 on Mondays and Tuesdays at 22:00 (KST) time slot for 32 episodes.
