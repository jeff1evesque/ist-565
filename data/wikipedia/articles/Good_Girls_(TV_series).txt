Good Girls is an American crime comedy-drama television series created by Jenna Bans that premiered on NBC on February 26, 2018. The series is executive produced by Bans, Dean Parisot (who directed the pilot) and Jeannine Renshaw for Universal Television. The first season ended on April 30, 2018. On May 7, 2018, NBC renewed the series for a second season. The series was released on Netflix internationally on July 4, 2018.
