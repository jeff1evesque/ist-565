The Keepers is a seven-episode American documentary web series that was released on Netflix on May 19, 2017 and was directed by Ryan White.The series explores the unsolved murder of nun Sister Cathy Cesnik, who taught English and drama at Baltimore's Archbishop Keough High School, and her former students' belief that there was a cover-up by authorities after Cesnik suspected that a priest at the school, A. Joseph Maskell, was guilty of sexual abuse of students.
