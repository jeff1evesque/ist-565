Bharat Ane Nenu (English: I, Bharat) is a 2018 Indian Telugu-language political thriller film written and directed by Koratala Siva, starring Mahesh Babu, Kiara Advani and Prakash Raj in the lead roles. The film is about Bharat, a student who unexpectedly becomes the leader of Andhra Pradesh and his attempts to reform politics. The film was released on 20 April 2018 to positive reviews and became the 3rd highest-grossing Telugu movie of all time. The film's dubbed versions were later released in Tamil as Bharat Ennum Naan and in Malayalam as Bharat Ennum Njan on 31 May 2018.
