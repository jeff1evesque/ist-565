Scarlett Sigourney Moffatt (born 17 October 1990) is an English reality television personality and presenter from County Durham, first known for appearing in the Channel 4 series Gogglebox. Before this she featured as a cast member in MTV's Beauty School Cop Outs. She won the sixteenth series of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! in 2016, and has since appeared a number of programmes, including the I'm a Celebrity companion show Extra Camp as a co-presenter, and Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway.
