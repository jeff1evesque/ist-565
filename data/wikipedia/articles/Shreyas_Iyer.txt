Shreyas Santosh Iyer (born 6 December 1994) is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Mumbai cricket team and plays One Day International and Twenty20 Internationals for the India national cricket team. He is a right-handed batsman and right-arm leg breakbowler. He played for the India Under-19 cricket team at the 2014 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup. He is the captain of Delhi Daredevils in the Indian Premier League.
