Neve Adrianne Campbell (; born October 3, 1973) is a Canadian actress. She is best known for her role as Sidney Prescott in the horror film franchise Scream. Campbell got her start appearing in the Canadian television drama series Catwalk (1992), before she played Julia Salinger in the American drama series Party of Five (1994–2000). Campbell played LeAnn Harvey in the Netflix drama series House of Cards (2016–17). She starred in other films, such as The Craft (1996), Wild Things (1998), Panic (2000), The Company (2003) and Skyscraper (2018).
