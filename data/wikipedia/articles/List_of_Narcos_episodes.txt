Narcos is an American crime web television series created and produced by Chris Brancato, Carlo Bernard, and Doug Miro. Season 1, comprising 10 episodes, originally aired on August 28, 2015, as a Netflix exclusive.Set and filmed in Colombia, Seasons 1 and 2 tell the story of notorious drug kingpin Pablo Escobar, who became a billionaire through the production and distribution of cocaine, while also focusing on Escobar's interactions with drug lords, DEA agents, and various opposition entities. Season 3 picks up after the fall of Escobar and continues to follow the DEA as they go up against the rise of the infamous Cali Cartel. The series was renewed for a second season, which premiered on September 2, 2016, and had 10 episodes. On September 6, 2016, Netflix renewed the series for a third and fourth season.
