Paisa Vasool (English: Money recovery or collection) is a 2017 Telugu action film produced by V. Anand Prasad under Bhavya Creations banner and directed by Puri Jagannadh. Starring Nandamuri Balakrishna, Shriya Saran, Musskan Sethi in the lead roles. Vikramjeet Virk plays the key role and music composed by Anup Rubens. The film marks Nandamuri Balakrishna's 101st appearance on the big screens and he has first time crooned a special song in this film with his own voice. The film received average response from critics and audience mostly for being predictable. The film was successful commercially.
The film was later dubbed into Hindi under the same name by Goldmines Telefilms and released on YouTube on 12 August 2018 with Nandamuri Balakrishna's voice dubbed by Amar Babaria.
