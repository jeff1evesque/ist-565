McMafia is a British crime drama television series created by Hossein Amini and James Watkins, and directed by Watkins. It is inspired by the book McMafia: A Journey Through the Global Criminal Underworld (2008) by journalist Misha Glenny. The series, which stars James Norton as Alex Godman, the British-raised son of a Russian mafia boss living in London whose father is trying to escape from the world of organised crime, is co-produced by the BBC, AMC, and Cuba Pictures. It was first broadcast on BBC One from 1 January 2018  and then premiered on AMC on 26 February 2018.Originally billed as a serial, BBC One renewed the show for an eight-chapter second series.
