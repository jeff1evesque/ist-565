Crazy Rich Asians is a 2013 novel by Kevin Kwan. 
Kwan stated that his intention in writing the novel was to "introduce a contemporary Asia to a North American audience." He claimed the novel was loosely based on his own childhood in Singapore.The novel became a bestseller and was followed by two sequels, China Rich Girlfriend in 2015 and Rich People Problems in 2017.
A film adaptation of the novel was released on August 15, 2018.
