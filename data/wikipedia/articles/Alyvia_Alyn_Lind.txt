Alyvia Alyn Lind (born 2006/2007) is an American child actress. She is known for playing the roles of Faith Newman on the CBS daytime soap opera The Young and the Restless since 2011, and young 9-year-old Dolly Parton in the made-for-television movies Dolly Parton's Coat of Many Colors and Dolly Parton's Christmas of Many Colors: Circle of Love.
