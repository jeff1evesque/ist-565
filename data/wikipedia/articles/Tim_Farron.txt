Timothy James Farron (born 27 May 1970) is a British politician who was the Leader of the Liberal Democrats between July 2015 and July 2017. He announced his resignation on 14 June 2017 following the 2017 UK general election, remaining in his position until Vince Cable was elected on 20 July 2017.He is the Member of Parliament (MP) for Westmorland and Lonsdale, having been elected in 2005 and re-elected in 2010, 2015 and 2017, and was the President of the Liberal Democrats from 2011 until 2014.
