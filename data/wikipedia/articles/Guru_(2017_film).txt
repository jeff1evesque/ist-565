Guru (English: Mentor) is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language sports drama film, produced by S. Shashikanth on Y NOT Studios banner and directed by Sudha Kongara. Starring Daggubati Venkatesh, Ritika Singh in the lead roles and music composed by Santhosh Narayanan, the film is remake of Kongara's own Tamil-Hindi bilingual film Irudhi Suttru (Saala Khadoos in Hindi) released in previous year which starred Ritika Singh along with R. Madhavan in lead roles. The film will be released in its Hindi dubbed version under the same name by Goldmines Telefilms in 2018.
