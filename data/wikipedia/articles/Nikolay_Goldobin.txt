Nikolay Sergeevich Goldobin (Николай Сергеевич Голдобин, born 7 October 1995) is a Russian professional ice hockey right winger for the Vancouver Canucks of the National Hockey League (NHL). He has previously played in the Liiga with HIFK and the American Hockey League with the Utica Comets. Goldobin was selected by the San Jose Sharks in the first round (27th overall) of the 2014 NHL Entry Draft.
