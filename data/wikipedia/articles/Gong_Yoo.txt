Gong Ji-cheol (Hangul: 공지철; born July 10, 1979), better known by his stage name Gong Yoo (Hangul: 공유), is a South Korean actor. He is best known for his roles in television dramas The 1st Shop of Coffee Prince (2007) and Guardian: The Lonely and Great God (2016–2017), and the films Silenced (2011),  Train to Busan (2016) and The Age of Shadows (2016).
