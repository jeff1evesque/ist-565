Naa Peru Surya, Naa Illu India (English: My Name is Surya and My Home is India) is a 2018 Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by Vakkantham Vamsi in his directorial debut. Produced by Shirisha and Sridhar Lagadapati under the banner Ramalakshmi Cine Creations, it stars Allu Arjun and Anu Emmanuel in the lead roles.
The film was released on 4 May 2018 along with its dubbed versions simultaneously in Tamil as En Peyar Surya, En Veedu India and in Malayalam as Ente Peru Surya Ente Veedu India.Later a Hindi-dubbed version titled Surya: The Brave Soldier will be released in selected theaters on 24 August 2018.
