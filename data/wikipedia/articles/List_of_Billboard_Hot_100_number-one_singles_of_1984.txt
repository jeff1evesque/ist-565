These are the Billboard Hot 100 number one hits of 1984. "Jump" by Van Halen and "When Doves Cry" by Prince each totaled five weeks at number one, the most of any song within 1984. However, "Like a Virgin" by Madonna had the longest run at number one of any song which rose into the top position during 1984. Though it spent only two weeks at number one at the end of the year, it went on to spend an additional four weeks at the top to begin 1985, for a total of six weeks. Overall, Prince spent the most weeks at number one in 1984, reigning for seven weeks at the top with "When Doves Cry" and "Let's Go Crazy" (with the Revolution).
