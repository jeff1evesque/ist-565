Secret City is an Australian drama television series that aired on Foxtel's Showcase. The six-part miniseries is based on the novels The Marmalade Files and The Mandarin Code by Chris Uhlmann and Steve Lewis. The series is written by Belinda Chayko, Matt Cameron and Greg Waters. It is produced by Joanna Werner.A second season, Secret City: Under the Eagle, began filming in February 2018.
