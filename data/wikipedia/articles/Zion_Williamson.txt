Zion Lateef Williamson (born July 6, 2000) is an American college basketball player for the Duke Blue Devils in the Atlantic Coast Conference (ACC). Listed at 6 ft 7 in (2.01 m) and 285 pounds (129 kg), he plays the power forward position. He is considered one of the top prospects for the 2019 NBA draft.
Williamson attended Spartanburg Day School in Spartanburg, South Carolina, where he was a five-star recruit and was ranked among the top players in his class. He led his team to multiple state titles during his high school career. Williamson was named McDonald's All-American and South Carolina Mr. Basketball as a senior in 2018. He gained national recognition in high school for his highlight videos featuring his reputed slam dunking ability.
