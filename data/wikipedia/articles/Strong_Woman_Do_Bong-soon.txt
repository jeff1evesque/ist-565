Strong Girl Bong-soon (Hangul: 힘쎈여자 도봉순; RR: Himssenyeoja Do Bong-sun; lit. Strong Woman Do Bong-soon) is a 2017 South Korean television series starring Park Bo-young in the title role as a woman with superhuman strength, with Park Hyung-sik and Ji Soo. It aired every Friday and Saturday on JTBC starting from February 24, 2017 to April 15, 2017.The series was a commercial hit and became one of the highest rated Korean dramas in cable television history.
