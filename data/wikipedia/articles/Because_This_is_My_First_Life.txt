Because This is My First Life (Hangul: 이번 생은 처음이라; RR: Ibeon Saengeun Cheoeumira; lit. This Life is Our First) is a 2017 South Korean television series starring Lee Min-ki and Jung So-min about different points of view on careers, relationships and marriage. The series marks Lee Min-ki's first small screen lead role since 2007. It aired on tvN every Monday and Tuesday from October 9 to November 28, 2017 at 21:30 KST.
