Sean Harris (born 8 February 1966) is an English actor and writer. He is known for his role as Solomon Lane in the films Mission: Impossible – Rogue Nation (2015) and Mission: Impossible – Fallout (2018).
Harris won a British Academy Television Award for Best Actor for his role in the miniseries Southcliffe (2013). He also received three consecutive BIFA Award for Best Supporting Actor nominations.
