It stars Rami Malek as Elliot Alderson, a cybersecurity engineer and hacker who has social anxiety disorder and clinical depression. Alderson is recruited by an insurrectionary anarchist known as "Mr. Robot", played by Christian Slater, to join a group of hacktivists. The group aims to cancel all debts by attacking the large conglomerate E Corp.
The pilot premiered on multiple online and video on demand services on May 27, 2015. As of  December 13, 2017, 32 episodes of Mr. Robot have aired, concluding the third season. On December 13, 2017, USA renewed Mr. Robot for a fourth season.
