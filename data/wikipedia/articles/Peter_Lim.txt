Peter Lim (born 1953) is a Singaporean business magnate, entrepreneur, philanthropist and investor. Lim, who was one of Singapore's leading stockbrokers, is now a private investor in sectors ranging from palm oil to medicine. In 2016, Forbes ranked him as the 11th richest person in Singapore with a net worth of US$2.4 billion.As a philanthropist, Lim focuses on education and sports, his most high-profile project being a $10 million scholarship fund to nurture local sports talents.Lim married Cherie Lim in 2003. He has two children with ex-wife Venus Teo Geok Fong.
