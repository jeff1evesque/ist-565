Season thirteen of the reality competition series America's Got Talent premiered on May 29, 2018, on NBC. Howie Mandel, Mel B, Heidi Klum and Simon Cowell returned as judges for their respective ninth, sixth, sixth, and third seasons. Meanwhile, Tyra Banks returned for her second season as host.The live shows return to the Dolby Theatre, beginning August 14, 2018.AGT is co-produced by FremantleMedia North America and Syco Entertainment, Cowell's company.Dunkin' Donuts is a sponsor for a fourth consecutive season.
