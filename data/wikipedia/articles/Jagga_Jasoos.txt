Jagga Jasoos (English: Detective Jagga) is a 2017 Indian musical mystery comedy adventure film written and directed by Anurag Basu, and produced by Basu and Ranbir Kapoor. The film features Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif  in lead roles, and tells the story of a teenage detective in search of his missing father. The film was released on 14 July 2017. A box office bomb, the film also received mixed reviews from critics.
