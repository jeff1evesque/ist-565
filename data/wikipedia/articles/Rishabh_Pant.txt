Rishabh Rajendra Pant (born 4 October 1997) is an Indian cricketer who plays for the India national cricket team and Delhi. In December 2015 he was named in India's squad for the 2016 Under-19 Cricket World Cup. On 1 February 2016, during the tournament, he hit an 18-ball fifty, the fastest at this level. He made his Twenty20 International (T20I) debut for India in January 2017 and his Test debut in August 2018.
