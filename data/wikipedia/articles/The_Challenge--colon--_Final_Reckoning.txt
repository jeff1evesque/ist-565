The Challenge: Final Reckoning is the thirty-second season of the MTV reality competition series The Challenge. This season featured alumni from Real World, Road Rules, The Challenge, Are You the One?, the U.S. version of Big Brother, and the U. K. television shows Ex on the Beach and Geordie Shore, competing along with alumni for the first time from Bad Girls Club and Vanderpump Rules who appeared on the U.S. version of Ex on the Beach. The season premiered on July 10, 2018 on MTV.
