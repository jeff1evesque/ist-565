Annadurai is a Tamil action family drama film directed by G. Srinivasan, starring Vijay Antony in the dual role. Vijay Antony also composed music for the film and also served as the film editor for the first time in his career. Produced by Fatima Vijay Antony and Raadhika, the film began production during February 2017. The film was dubbed in Telugu as Indrasena.
