James Geoffrey Ian Norton (born 18 July 1985) is an English actor. He is best known for roles in the British TV series Happy Valley, Grantchester, War & Peace, and McMafia. For his role as ex-convict Tommy Lee Royce in Happy Valley, Norton was nominated for the British Academy Television Award for Best Supporting Actor in 2015. Norton was educated at the University of Cambridge, where he read theology and performed in student theatre productions.
