Richard Madden (born 18 June 1986) is a Scottish actor. He gained recognition for playing Robb Stark in the first three seasons of the fantasy television HBO series Game of Thrones and Prince Kit in the Disney romantic fantasy film Cinderella (2015). Madden went on to portray Cosimo de' Medici in the drama series Medici: Masters of Florence (2016), starred in the Netflix romantic comedy Ibiza (2018), and has the leading role in Bodyguard (2018).
