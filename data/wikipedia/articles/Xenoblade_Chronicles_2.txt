Xenoblade Chronicles 2 is an action role-playing game developed by Monolith Soft and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch video game console. The game is part of the Xeno series, serving as a sequel to the first Xenoblade Chronicles, and was released worldwide on December 1, 2017. The game sold over a million copies within a month of its release. A large story-based downloadable content (DLC) addition, titled Xenoblade Chronicles 2: Torna ~ The Golden Country, is scheduled for release in September 2018.
